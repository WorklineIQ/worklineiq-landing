1. Who We Are

WorklineIQ ("we," "us," or "our") provides a cloud-based field production and billing intelligence platform for utility and fiber contractors. Our platform is accessible at worklineiq.com and through our web application.

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect when you use our website, platform, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). It does not apply to information our customers collect from their own end users — customers are responsible for their own privacy practices with respect to data they collect through our platform.

For questions about this policy, contact us at don@worklineiq.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account & Contact Information

When a company subscribes to WorklineIQ, we collect information about the company and its designated administrators, including:

  • Company name, address, and contact information
  • Administrator name, email address, and phone number
  • Billing contact and payment information (processed by our payment provider; we do not store full payment card details)

Platform Data

We collect and store data submitted by your organization through the platform, including:

  • Job records, location data, pay items, and contract information
  • Field production entries submitted by crew members
  • Approval records, pay application data, and invoices
  • Equipment records, purchase orders, and materials data
  • Photos attached to field submissions

This data belongs to your organization. See Section 5 (Data Ownership) for details.

Usage & Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use our Services, including:

  • IP addresses and device identifiers
  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, features used, and actions taken within the platform
  • Error logs and performance data

This information is used to maintain, improve, and secure our platform.

Communications

If you contact us by email or through our website, we retain the content of those communications to respond to your inquiry and improve our support.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the WorklineIQ platform
  • Process transactions and send related communications (invoices, receipts, service notices)
  • Respond to support requests and customer inquiries
  • Monitor and analyze platform usage to improve performance and features
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent security incidents and fraudulent activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data to serve advertisements. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your platform data to train AI models or build products for third parties.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers (Subprocessors)

We use a limited number of third-party providers to help deliver our Services. These providers access your data only to perform services on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations. Our primary infrastructure providers include:

  • Supabase — database hosting, authentication, and storage (SOC 2 Type II certified)
  • Vercel — frontend hosting and content delivery

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of WorklineIQ, our customers, or others.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of our assets, customer data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify affected customers prior to any such transfer.

With Your Consent

We may share information in other circumstances with your explicit consent.

5. Data Ownership & Portability

Your data is yours Customer organizations own all data submitted to WorklineIQ. We hold it as a processor on your behalf. You can export your data at any time, and we will return or delete it upon termination of your account.

You may export your company's data at any time using the export functionality within the platform. Upon termination of your account, we will make your data available for export for 30 days, after which it will be deleted from our systems.

We do not claim any ownership interest in your field data, contract data, job records, or any other customer-submitted content.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your devices and our servers is encrypted via TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Encryption at rest: All stored data is encrypted using AES-256, managed by Supabase at the infrastructure level.
  • Row-Level Security: Our database enforces strict data isolation — your company's data is never accessible to other customers at the database level.
  • Authentication: Admin and office users authenticate via Supabase Auth with JWT tokens. Field crew access uses server-side PIN validation.
  • Access controls: Role-based permissions with 25+ granular flags limit access to only what each user role requires.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you have reason to believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at don@worklineiq.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon termination:

  • Customer data remains available for export for 30 days following termination.
  • After 30 days, customer data is deleted from our production systems.
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days before being fully purged.
  • Billing records and communications may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and accounting purposes.

8. Cookies & Tracking

Our platform uses a minimal set of cookies and local storage necessary to operate the service, including:

  • Authentication tokens: To keep you logged in during your session.
  • Session preferences: To remember your in-app settings.
  • Offline queue: Field entry data is temporarily stored in IndexedDB on your device when offline. This data is synced and cleared upon logout.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not participate in cross-site behavioral advertising networks.

Our website may use basic analytics (such as page view counts) to understand how visitors use worklineiq.com. If we add analytics tools, we will update this policy accordingly.

9. Field Crew Users

WorklineIQ supports field crew access via QR code and PIN — no email address or personal account is required for field-level users. Field crew accounts are created and managed by the subscribing company (the employer), not by individual crew members.

Field crew data — including daily production entries, locations, and photos — is submitted on behalf of the employer and is governed by the employer's use of the platform. Crew members should direct any questions about their data to their employer.

WorklineIQ does not independently collect or use field crew personal data beyond what is submitted through the platform by the employer.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights with respect to your personal information, including the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to our retention obligations)
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at don@worklineiq.com. We will respond within 30 days. Note that many of these rights are fulfilled at the account level — company administrators can access, correct, and export their organization's data directly through the platform.

WorklineIQ is operated from the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

11. Children's Privacy

WorklineIQ is a business-to-business platform intended for use by companies and their employees. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify active customers by email and update the effective date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Services after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
