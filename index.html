How It Works

Four steps. Zero spreadsheets.
Billing by end of day.

Set up your job once. Crews log work from the field in under two minutes. The office approves. Billing generates automatically from approved work × your rates. That's it.

1
Set up your job once

Add your line items, rates, and locations. One-time setup per job. Everything connects automatically.

2
Crews log from the field

Tap a location, log what was done, snap a photo. Under 2 minutes. Works offline. Syncs when signal returns.

3

Field entries land in a review queue. Compare actuals to plan, review photos, approve or reject. One click per entry.

4
Billing writes itself

Approved work × your rates = billing-ready output. Select a date range, generate, and send. No re-entry. No formulas. No mistakes.
