The Problem

Billing errors run both directions —
and most contractors can't prove either one

When field data lives in text threads, paper tickets, and memory, your invoice is only as accurate as whoever reconstructs it. Miss a pay item and you're underbilling. Round a quantity up and you're exposed on disputes. Push back on a line item without structured records and you're negotiating from memory, not evidence.

📋
Pay items that never make the invoice Work gets logged in a text or on a ticket and never makes it to billing. Nobody catches it until the check is short.
✏️
Quantities that get rounded the wrong way Manual re-entry from field tickets means estimating. Those estimates almost never round in your favor.
⚠️
Disputes you can't defend When a client pushes back on a line item, paper tickets and text threads aren't backup — they're evidence problems.
⏳
Cash sitting idle between billing cycles When compiling an invoice takes days of chasing and re-entry, money that should be collected keeps waiting.

Where work disappears

The gap between what your crews do in the field and what ends up on an invoice is where revenue leaks. It's not a billing problem — it's a data capture problem.

You can't bill what you can't prove

Clients dispute line items. Contracts have specific pay item requirements. Without structured, timestamped field records, you're negotiating instead of invoicing.

How It Works

Structured data from the field.
Clean invoices from the office.

WorklineIQ gives your crews a simple field app tied to your actual job pay items and contract rates. They log quantities. You approve. The invoice calculates itself. No spreadsheets. No re-entry. No short billing.

Load your job pay items and rates

Approved quantities × contract rates = invoice line items. Generate billing-ready output without touching a spreadsheet.
