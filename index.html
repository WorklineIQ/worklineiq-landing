How It Works

Simple for crews.
Effortless for the office.

WorklineIQ gives your crews a simple mobile app tied to your actual job pay items. They log what they did. You approve it. Pay apps generate automatically. The whole process takes minutes, not days.

1
Load your job pay items and rates

Upload your job matrix once. Pay items, contract rates, and locations are structured automatically — no manual setup on each job.

2
Crews log actuals from the field

Simple mobile app. Crews select the location, enter quantities against your actual pay items, and attach photos. Works offline.

3
Review and approve in the office

Submissions hit an approval queue. Review actuals against planned quantities, flag questions, and approve in one step.

4
Pay app generates itself

Approved quantities × contract rates = pay app line items. Generate billing-ready output without touching a spreadsheet.
