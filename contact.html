Common Questions

How long does a demo take?

About 30 minutes. We cover field entry capture, the approval workflow, and pay app generation. If you want to go deeper on a specific area — equipment tracking, reporting, crew setup — we can extend or schedule a follow-up.

What does WorklineIQ cost?

We price on a flat annual fee by user tier — not per crew seat. Packages start at the Essential level and scale up to Professional and Executive. Contact us for a quote based on your team size and needs. Pilot pricing is available for a 90-day trial.

How long does implementation take?

Typically 2–4 weeks from contract signing to go-live. We handle the configuration, data import, and crew onboarding alongside you. Most customers run a 2-week parallel period before fully switching over. See our implementation overview for the full picture.

Do field crews need email addresses or personal accounts?

No. Field crew access works via QR code and PIN — no email address required. Crews are typically set up and submitting same-day. Admin and office users have standard accounts.

Where is WorklineIQ based?

Wisconsin. We work with utility and fiber contractors across the Midwest and beyond.
